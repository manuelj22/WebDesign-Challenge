Cloudiness VS Latitude

The cloudiness in a city is plotted here as a function of the city's latitude. It seems that there is a bigger number of cities with 0% cloudiness, and cities with almost 100% cloudiness. Although we cannot be entirely certain (because the data looks evenly distributed), it seems that cities in the northern hemisphere are cloudier.
